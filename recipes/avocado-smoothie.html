Avocado Smoothie Recipe

Description

This avocado smoothie is thick, creamy, and delicious. Makes a great summertime smoothie.

It takes approximately 5 minutes to prepare for 2 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 ripe avocado, halved and pitted
  • Â½ cup vanilla yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 8 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place milk, avocado, yogurt, and honey into a blender; add ice cubes and blend until smooth.
