Easy Mango Banana Smoothie Recipe

Description

This mango banana smoothie is great for kids. As a babysitter, I always look for quick, easy, healthy, and delicious recipes.

It takes approximately 10 minutes to prepare for 8 servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded, and sliced
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 cups vanilla yogurt
  • 2 cups milk

Steps

  1. Combine mangos, banana, vanilla yogurt, and milk in a blender; blend until smooth.
