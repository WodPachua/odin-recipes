APineapple Cleanser Smoothie Recipe

Description

An energizing cucumber and pineapple smoothie with coconut water, ginger, and lemon.

It takes approximately 10 minutes to prepare for 1 serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut water, or to taste
  • 1 cup chopped fresh pineapple
  • 1 stalk celery
  • ½ cucumber, peeled
  • ½ lemon, peeled
  • ⅓ bunch fresh parsley
  • 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger root

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Blend coconut water, pineapple, celery, cucumber, lemon, parsley, and ginger together in a blender until smooth.
